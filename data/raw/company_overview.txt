Company Overview – Antigravity Electronics

Antigravity Electronics is a demo online electronics store focused on a small but high-quality catalog of devices. 
We currently specialise in:

- Laptops for students, professionals, and business users
- Wireless headphones for music, calls, and entertainment

Our primary goal is to make it simple for customers to:

- Discover the right laptop or headphones for their use case
- Place and track orders online
- Request returns, refunds, and warranty support when needed

Product categories we focus on:

1. Laptops:
   - Lightweight laptops for students and remote workers
   - Business-focused laptops for office and productivity use

2. Headphones:
   - Wireless noise-cancelling headphones
   - Budget-friendly wireless headphones with strong bass

Customer support scope:

Our support assistant can help customers with:
- Order status and tracking
- Return and refund eligibility
- Warranty information
- Basic troubleshooting for devices (for example, “laptop not turning on” or “headphones no sound”)
- Product comparisons and suggestions based on budget and use case

Order ID format:

Orders in our system follow the format ORD1234, for example:
- ORD1001
- ORD1002
- ORD1003

This format is used throughout our tools and backend system for order lookups.

Demo / educational note:

This store and its data are designed for educational and demo purposes. All orders, products, and policies in this system are synthetic examples to test a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) assistant for ecommerce support.
